About Me

Summary

After fulfilling a 3 year and 27 week contract with the US Army, I went to the University with a GI Bill and left with a bachelor's degree in English Literature. Instead of getting a job as a copywriter or editor, I continued my education by completing FCC's front end certificate.

Currently, I work in the .NET world using C#, building websites in ASP.NET and processes that streamline company workflow. I have worked on trading applications such as Kraken and Interactive Brokers (among others) and built many features for accounting software.

During my free time I mess around with software languages, frameworks, libraries and some of the many features on AWS. At heart, I am a builder and a fiddler. I find the best way to learn is to throw things together, see what happens.
